Got Questions? We've Got Answers!

If these commonly asked questions don't cover your concerns, email us at questions@shiftmates.net

  • Can the schedule be accessed offline?

    The most up to date schedule can be accessed anywhere one of your devices has a network connection.

  • How can employees trade shifts?

    Any scheduled employee can post their shift to the shift-marketplace where another authorized user can claim it.

  • What is included in the SMS interface?

    Employees can access all functionality of viewing, trading, and managing their own shifts via text message keywords.

  • Is Email support enough for me?

    Our support technicians are trained experts. Satisfaction is guaranteed in 48 hours.

  • What software integrations does ShiftMates support?

    Click here to see all of the ShiftMates Zapier integrations.

  • How does Cooperative Coverage work?

    Simply grant a trusted company access to your shifts. Once the agreement has been confirmed by both parties, employees will be able to claim shifts from either shift marketplace. Don't worry, there are plenty of permission granularities to meet your exact needs.

  • What is included in Advanced Customization?

    All software integrations and data exporting, plus the ability to create custom commands in the SMS interface.

  • Do I need the Elite Support?

    Elite Support includes up to 4 hours of on-site training and setup for your business. All issues reported by your organization will be automatically prioritized in our system.
